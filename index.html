Lemmy and PieFed. Simplified.

Summit makes browsing Lemmy and PieFed effortless so you can focus on the content, not the complexities.

Fully customizable

Almost every aspect of Summit can be tweaked to give you a personalized experience.

All settings are searchable, making personalization effortless.

Powerful "meta-features" let you to save, restore or share your configuration so testing different setups is easy.

Create, edit & draft

Post or comment with ease using Summit's powerful content creation tools. Save drafts of posts or comments to submit later.

Drafts are automatically saved so your work is never lost.

Lemmy & PieFed support


Summit is committed to supporting both Lemmy and PieFed. So you never have to choose between them.

Mod & Admin actions


Moderate communities and instance right from the app. All moderation and admin actions are supported.
